Wildlife Safety Guide

Stay Calm and Observe

  • Keep a safe distance from wild animals.
  • Observe behavior quietly without disturbing them.
  • Use binoculars or cameras to record for awareness.

Do NOT Interfere

  • Do not touch, feed, or chase the animal.
  • Avoid loud noises or sudden movements.
  • Do not attempt to move or relocate the animal unless instructed by authorities.

Report and Seek Help

  • Call local wildlife helpline or forest officer immediately.
  • Provide clear information: location, type of animal, condition.
  • If safe, take photos/videos to help rescuers.

Educate Yourself

  • Learn about wildlife behavior in your area.
  • Understand which species are dangerous and which are endangered.
  • Participate in wildlife awareness programs and campaigns.

Promote Safety & Conservation

  • Share safe wildlife practices with friends and family.
  • Support conservation initiatives like volunteering or donations.
  • Spread awareness on social media responsibly.
